Popular places

Kyrgyzstan

Batken

Batken is a region of Kyrgyzstan. The northern part of the region is part of the agricultural Ferghana Valley.
Kyrgyzstan

Issyk Kul Lake

The lake surrounded two sides by majestic mountain ranges Kungey and Terskey Ala-Too is known as Issyk Kul, which in Kyrgyz means "hot lake".
Kyrgyzstan

Eki Naryn Valley

Eki Naryn Valley is a beautiful place with rugged rivers and coniferous forests at an altitude of about 2000 meters.
Kyrgyzstan

Zhashyl Kol Lake

Zhashyl Kol Lake, which from Kyrghyz means "green lake", is located in Chon-Kemin valley, at an altitude of about 3200 m.
Kyrgyzstan

Inylchek Glacier

Engilchek Glacier is a glacier in the Central Tian Shan Mountains of Issyk-Kul Region, northeastern Kyrgyzstan.
Kyrgyzstan

Teshik Kul Lake

The Teshik Kul Lake is located at the end of Karakaman valley at an altitude of 3500 m.
Kyrgyzstan

Gumbez Manas

Gumbez of Manas is a rare kind of structure made from burnt bricks and carved terracotta. The architecture of the mausoleum is of portal type.
Kyrgyzstan

Besh Tash Lake

Highland Lake Besh Tash is located in the eponymous valley at an altitude of 2997 m. The depth of the lake reaches 28 m.
Kyrgyzstan

Chunkurchak Gorge

The Chunkurchak Gorge is located between the Alamedin and Ala Archa gorges and forms a large hollow. From the east, it borders on the canyons. Alamedin, and from the west with Malinovoe Gorge (in the lower part) and with Ala Archa (in the upper part).
Kyrgyzstan

Dolon Pass

The height of the Dolon Pass is 3038 meters above sea level. It runs only from June to October.
